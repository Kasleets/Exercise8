FAQ

Is your coffee sustainably sourced?

Yes, all our coffee is sourced from farms that use sustainable farming practices. We believe in preserving our environment for future generations.

Do you practice fair trade?

Absolutely! We ensure that all our suppliers adhere to fair trade practices, ensuring that farmers get a fair price for their produce.

Who prepares the coffee?

Our experienced baristas are trained to prepare the perfect cup of coffee every single time. They are passionate about coffee and have years of experience in the field.

Are you just another corporate entity?

No, we are a team of coffee lovers who came together to share our love for coffee. We are passionate about bringing the best coffee to our customers.

Is it true this is a startup by young people?

Yes, Bean Queen was started by a group of young individuals passionate about coffee. We believe in innovation, quality, and outstanding service.
